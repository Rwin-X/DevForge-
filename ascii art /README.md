## Overview

ASCII.GEN transforms ordinary images into detailed ASCII artwork directly inside your browser.

Unlike many online converters, all processing happens locally on your machine. Your images never leave your device.

Whether you're creating terminal art, retro graphics, hacker-style visuals, or simply experimenting with ASCII aesthetics, ASCII.GEN provides a fast and modern workflow.
